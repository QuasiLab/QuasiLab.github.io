High-Energy Physics Computation

This research group are working on experimental nuclear and particle physics in Indonesia. Our group is a member of ALICE (A Large Ion Collider Experiment) Collaboration at CERN. The LIPI ALICE Team is active in grid/high performance computing, the ALICE O2 (Online-Offline) Computing Upgrade, ALICE ITS (Inner Tracking System) Upgrade, and heavy-flavour physics analysis. Outside high-energy physics, other area of interest of our group are statistical data analysis, machine learning, software engineering, high performance computing, and scientific instrumentation.
